About Me

Academic Profile

Dhanushka Nuwan is an enthusiastic and dedicated criminology lecturer with teaching experience in higher education.

He is passionate about student-centered learning, curriculum development, research, academic mentoring, and preparing students for meaningful careers in criminology and criminal justice. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, he combines theoretical knowledge with practical insights to create engaging and transformative learning experiences for his students.

His research interests span across criminological theories, children's rights, drug-related studies, Buddhist teachings in justice, crime prevention, and contemporary social issues. He actively contributes to scholarly discourse through publications and conference presentations.
